Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this site is to promote our services to adventurous people who loves exploring nature and as well loves exercising the body. This activity is not just for the young single adults, rather it is a recreational activity that is open to the whole family provided they are ready to have fun. There will be detailed information about what you need to expect on your adventure and when to make a schedule. It will also include history of the raft guides in taking out the troop for rafting.

Audience

The ideal customer for Splash are athletic families, individuals and as well groups who are interested in outdoor adventures that will create an opportunity to explore nature and as well get cozy with each other. The site is available to every person from the age of 14 and above provided there is an access to either a desktop, a laptop, or a mobile device.
